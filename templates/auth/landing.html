UFAA Recruitment Portal

Welcome to the official recruitment portal of the Unclaimed Financial Assets Authority (UFAA).
The Unclaimed Financial Assets Authority (UFAA) is a state corporation established under the National Treasury pursuant to the Unclaimed Financial Assets Act. No. 40 of 2011. The primary mandate of the Authority is to receive unclaimed financial assets from holders of such assets, safeguard these unclaimed assets, and re-unite the assets with their rightful owners.

Application Steps

1. Register and Login

Create an account using a valid email address, then log in with your email and password.

2. Complete Your Profile

Ensure your profile is complete and accurate by adding:

  • Personal information
  • Academic qualifications (KCSE to PhD, where applicable)
  • Professional qualifications and certifications
  • Membership to professional bodies (where applicable)
  • Work experience
  • National ID / Passport (for verification)
3. Apply for Vacancies

Once your profile is complete, open the vacancy you are interested in and submit your application. You may apply for more than one vacancy, provided you meet the requirements for each position.

Please review your information carefully before submitting. Incomplete or inaccurate information may affect the outcome of your application.

Important Notice: UFAA does not charge any fee at any stage of the recruitment process. Do not pay anyone claiming to offer recruitment assistance.
